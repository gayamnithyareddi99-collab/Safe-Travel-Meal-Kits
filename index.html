About Safe Travel Meal Kits

Safe Travel Meal Kits is the UK’s first itinerary-aware, AI-powered travel meal assurance platform designed specifically for travellers with severe food allergies and strict dietary needs.

Unlike traditional meal kit services that focus on fixed home delivery, our system converts confirmed travel itineraries into a fully coordinated “Safe Food Journey” — delivering sealed, allergen-safe meals to transient locations such as airports, hotels, and railway hubs.

Built on a tech-hospitality fusion model, the platform integrates itinerary-driven scheduling, severity-aware multi-allergen logic, GPS-timed delivery coordination, and QR-based traceability to create an accountable end-to-end safety system.

How The Safe Food Journey Works

A structured, itinerary-driven system ensuring allergen-safe meals are delivered exactly when and where travelers need them.

01

Create Your Allergy Profile

Travelers enter detailed dietary requirements including severe allergens, intolerances, religious restrictions, and severity levels. The system builds a safety-first profile.

02

Sync Your Travel Itinerary

Upload or connect your flight and hotel details. The platform converts your journey into scheduled “safe meal points” across departure, arrival, and hotel stay.

03

Allergen-Safe Kitchen Preparation

Partner kitchens follow strict HACCP and multi-layer allergen SOPs. Each meal is sealed, labeled, and verified before dispatch.

04

GPS-Timed Delivery

Meals are delivered precisely to airport landside zones, hotel receptions, or designated travel points based on real-time arrival schedules.

05

QR Safety Passport Verification

Each kit includes a QR traceability summary showing ingredient verification, kitchen sign-off, and chain-of-custody confirmation.

06

Safe & Stress-Free Travel

Travelers enjoy their journey without last-minute food risk, replacing uncertainty with a verified and structured Safe Food Journey.
